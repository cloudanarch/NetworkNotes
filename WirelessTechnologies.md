## Infrared

- Data Transitted in Rays of Light rather than Radio Waves
- Many products use this
- Requires Direct line of Sight. 
- Doesn't work in Direct Sunlight. (weakened or blocked)

## Bluetooth

- Short Range Radio
- Between Devices.
- 2.4 Ghz
- Voice AND Data
- Xfer 24Mbps 
- 100m (PAN)
